Reduced missed apponitments in Outpatient Clinic

Overview

A busy outpatient clinic faced growing operational strain despite steady patient demand. High appointment volumes were undermined by frequent no-shows and inefficient scheduling processes, leading to underutilized clinical time and inconsistent patient flow.

The Challenge

Manual booking and reminder processes caused missed appointments, poor schedule visibility, and increased administrative workload, resulting in underutilized time and revenue loss.

Our Solutions
